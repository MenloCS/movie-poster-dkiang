# Movie Poster Project

You will design a movie poster step by step, using AI as a creative tool—not a shortcut.  
At each step, save and commit your work in GitHub Desktop.

## Using GitHub Desktop (Mac)

1. **Edit** this file (`movie-poster-project.md`) in TextEdit.  
2. **Save** your changes.  
3. Open **GitHub Desktop**.  
   - Check that `movie-poster-project.md` shows up under **Changes**.  
   - Write a commit summary (see below).  
   - Click **Commit to main**.  
4. Click **Push origin** (top bar) to upload to GitHub.com.  

**Commit Messages for Each Step**
- Step 1: *Completed creative brief*  
- Step 2: *Documented first two AI attempts*  
- Step 3: *Documented final AI attempts* 
- Step 4: *Added Photoshop modifications* 
- Step 5: *Completed reflection*

## Step 2: AI Generation – First Attempts
*(Commit message: "Documented first two AI attempts")*

Open Adobe Firefly. Generate images based on your creative brief, but remember: you're exploring possibilities, not looking for a final answer.

**Attempt 1:**  
- **My prompt:**  A large spherical space station, starry black space background, green rays emerging from the space station and converging on a point.

- **What worked:** I got a pretty good design for a space station, it doesn't look exactly like the Death Star but that's ok
- **What didn't work:** The green rays don't come out and converge on a single point.
- **What I'll change for next attempt:**  
I need to be more specific about how the green rays start and end.

**Attempt 2:**  
- **My prompt:**  A large spherical space station, starry black space background, 5 green rays emerge from opposite sides of a circle and converge on a single point to the side of the space station.

- **What worked:** One of the versions looks right, with the green rays 
- **What didn't work:** They don't want to converge
- **What I'll change for next attempt:** Simplify this by making it just one ray
